NOx Emissions

The NOx emissions value indicates how much nitrogen oxides the tested vehicle emitted per kilometer during the RDE drive.

These values differ depending on the year of type approval and the year of first admission of the vehicle.

Diesel powered vehicle types approved between September 2017 and December 2019, or vehicles firstly admitted between September 2019 and December 2020, must emit at most 168 mg/km of nitric oxides. Diesel cars approved or admitted later, must conform to a 120 mg/km threshold.
